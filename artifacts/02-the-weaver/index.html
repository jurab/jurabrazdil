This ball of entangled strings,
like a cobweb folded onto itself
sits in my stomach, my chest and my mind.
I've been turning it around for so long
it feels quite familiar,
it stretches back throughout the past.
Turning my back to the future
I can see the mingle unfold and stretch behind me
through time like a vine I am hanging on,
slowly moving backwards into the unknown
and like a spider I am weaving the rope
to support the weight of my soul.
Hanging in the void,
strands extending back up into the darkness of the past.
In the distance to my sides there are figures,
figures obscured by mist and vapour,
suspended on webs of their own.
